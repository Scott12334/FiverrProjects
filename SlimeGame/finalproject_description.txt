This game is a simple obstacle avoidance game.
The player runs around the screen using the WASD keys and avoids getting hit by the moving slime.
There are sound effects in the game, and you can mute them and unmute them just by hitting the space bar.
There is a beginging screen and a game over screen.
The first screen showcases the highest score that is stored in the score.txt file.
The game over screen gives the player the chance to reset the game.

The player class simply controls the character and movement. 
The obstacle class controls the movement of the slimes after they spawn. There spawning is handled by a method in the main class.
